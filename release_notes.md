# World Time Specialist 1.0.0

## English

### Highlights

- Base time auto/manual with full DST support.
- IANA timezone list with autocomplete and city lookup.
- Universal converter, two‑city comparison and education tab.
- World map with UTC bands, live markers and day/night shading.
- Time tiles dashboard with drag/resize/snap and full‑screen mode.
- Market sessions with activity status and sound cues.
- Alarms, timers and stopwatch with scripts and daily repeat.
- Tray integration, autostart options and settings persistence.

### Assets

- `WorldTimeSpecialist-Portable.exe`
- `WorldTimeSpecialist-Setup.exe`
- `build-scripts.zip` (build scripts + NSIS + spec)
- `checksums.txt`
